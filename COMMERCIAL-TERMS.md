A separate agreement is required for commercial use of the BSL Core while its
BSL restrictions apply, and for any commercial use of the Agentic Canvas
product package. Examples include:

1. **Paid software or resale** — distributing, sublicensing, or embedding the
   Core or Canvas in a paid product or commercial offering.
2. **Hosted service / SaaS** — operating Canvas functionality, managed rendering,
   API access, or a substantially similar hosted workflow for commercial benefit.
3. **Commercial deployment** — deploying the Canvas in a commercial business
   workflow or as a paid internal capability.
4. **Premium assets or grammar packs** — using separately identified proprietary
   assets, curated grammar packs, or other commercial material outside its
