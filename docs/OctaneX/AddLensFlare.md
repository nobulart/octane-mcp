### Lens Flare Parameters

Enabled - Determines whether the effect is active or not.

Strength - The amount of lens flare to apply. 0% means no lens flare and 100% means the lens flare is as.strong as the orginal image.

Extent - Controls the overall length and distances between lens flare highlights.
