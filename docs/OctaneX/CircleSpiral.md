### Circle Spiral Parameters

Primary Color - The primary color of the circles.

Secondary Color - The secondary color of the circles, a blending occurs between the primary and secondary colors.

Circle Count - The number of circles to be generated in the spiral.

Spiral Radius - The radius of the entire spiral shape.

Circle Radius - Set the size or radius of the circles shapes.

Time - Animates the circle spiral effect.

UV Transform - Positions, scales, and rotates the surface texture.

Projection - Sets how the texture projects onto the surface.
