The Clamp texture provides a minimum and maximum value to clamp the values of an incoming texture map. In Figure 1, the material shows a Turbulence texture passing through a Clamp texture, and then it connects to the [Diffuse material](javascript:void(0);)\'s [Diffuse](javascript:void(0);) channel. The Clamp values are set to a minimum of 0.401 and maximum of 0.970 resulting in less contrast than the original Turbulence texture.
