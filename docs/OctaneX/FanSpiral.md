### Fan Spiral Parameters

Time - Animates the fan spiral effect.

Spin - Specifies the amount of spin or rotation for the fan gradients.

Arc Count - Determines the number of fan gradients.

UV Transform - Positions, scales, and rotates the surface texture.

Projection - Sets how the texture projects onto the surface.
