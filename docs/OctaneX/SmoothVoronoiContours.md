### Smooth Voronoi Contours Parameters

Frequency - Determines the base color underlying the other iridescent colors.

Time - The animation time stamp used to pan the textures across a surface.

[Glossy](javascript:void(0);) - Adjusts the color swatches to provide a glossy-friendly Voronoi pattern.

UVW Transform - Positions, scales, and rotates the surface texture.

Projection - Sets how the texture projects onto the surface.
