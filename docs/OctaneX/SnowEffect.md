### Snow Effect Parameters

Time - Provides control over animating the texture effect.

Radius - Determines the size of the snow flakes.

Flake Count - Specifies the number of snow flakes.

Blizzardness - Determines the direction and shape of the snow flakes.

UVW Transform - Positions, scales, and rotates the surface texture.

Projection - Sets how the texture projects onto the surface.
