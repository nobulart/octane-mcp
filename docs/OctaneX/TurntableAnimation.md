Figure 1: The Turntable Animation settings window

The Duration and Frames re-adjust when you change either of their value. Shutter Speed creates the motion blur effect, specifying the shutter time percentage relative to the duration of a single frame. Shutter Speed controls how much time the shutter stays open. You can set this parameter to any value above 100%.
